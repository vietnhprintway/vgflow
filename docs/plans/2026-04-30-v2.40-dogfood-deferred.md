## Concerns to surface during dogfood

- Real Gemini Flash subprocess timing — current default
  `worker_timeout_seconds=600`; production phases may hit timeout when MCP
  playwright bootstrap is slow.
- Round-robin across `playwright1..5` MCP slots assumes those profiles
  exist in `~/.gemini/settings.json`; the smoke fixture does not check.
- 6-rule eligibility leans on `disposable_seed_data: true` — production
  phases on staged data may need an override path that does not log
  OVERRIDE-DEBT critical for every run.
